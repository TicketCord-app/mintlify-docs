Customize your TicketCord experience with profile settings, notifications, themes, and more.

## Accessing Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Go to [ticketcord.net/dashboard](https://ticketcord.net/dashboard)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Profile">
    Click your avatar in the top right corner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Settings">
    Choose **Settings** from the dropdown
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Settings

### Basic Information

Your profile shows:

| Field | Source | Editable |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Avatar** | From Discord | No (syncs from Discord) |
| **Username** | From Discord | No (syncs from Discord) |
| **Email** | From Discord | Change in Discord |
| **Display Name** | Custom | Yes |

### Updating Profile

- **Display Name**: Your preferred name shown in the dashboard
- Profile syncs automatically from Discord
- To change avatar/username, update in Discord

## Notification Settings

### Email Notifications

Configure which emails you receive:

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Billing** | Payment confirmations, invoices |
| **Product Updates** | New features, announcements |
| **Security** | Login alerts, security notifications |
| **Weekly Summary** | Usage and performance digest |

### Dashboard Notifications

In-app notifications for:

- Ticket activity
- Bot status changes
- System alerts
- Feature tips

### Managing Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Notifications">
    Settings → Notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle Categories">
    Enable/disable notification types
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Changes apply immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Theme Settings

### Dark/Light Mode

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Light** | Bright background, dark text |
| **Dark** | Dark background, light text |
| **System** | Follows your OS preference |

### Changing Theme

1. Go to Settings → Appearance
2. Select your preferred theme
3. Theme applies immediately

<Tip>
  Use the keyboard shortcut to quickly toggle theme (shown in Settings).
</Tip>

## Privacy Settings

### Data Controls

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Export Data** | Download all your data |
| **Delete Account** | Permanently remove account |

### Export Your Data

Request a full export of your TicketCord data:

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Click **Export My Data**
3. Receive email when ready
4. Download within 7 days

See our [Privacy Policy](https://ticketcord.net/privacy) for more information.

### Delete Account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All bots, tickets, and data are permanently deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Danger Zone
2. Click **Delete My Account**
3. Read the confirmation message
4. Type your username to confirm
5. Click **Delete Permanently**

## Session Management

### Active Sessions

View where you're signed in:

### Signing Out

- **This device**: Click **Sign Out** in profile menu
- **All devices**: Settings → Security → Sign Out Everywhere

## Connected Services

### Discord Connection

Your Discord account is the primary authentication:

- Cannot be changed (sign in with different account to switch)
- Permissions requested during sign-in
- Revoke access in Discord settings if needed

### Revoking Access

To disconnect TicketCord from Discord:

1. Go to Discord → User Settings → Authorized Apps
2. Find TicketCord
3. Click **Deauthorize**

<Note>
  Revoking Discord access will sign you out of TicketCord but won't delete your data.
</Note>
