Manage all aspects of your TicketCord subscription from the billing dashboard.

## Accessing Billing Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Go to [ticketcord.net/dashboard](https://ticketcord.net/dashboard) and sign in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    Click on your profile icon in the top right, then select **Billing**
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Subscription">
    Your current plan, billing cycle, and next payment date are displayed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrading Your Plan

When you upgrade to a higher tier:

1. **Immediate Access**: New features are available instantly
2. **Prorated Charges**: You only pay the difference for the remaining billing period
3. **Same Billing Cycle**: Your billing date stays the same

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    From the billing page, click **Change Plan** and select your desired tier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    See what new features you'll gain and the prorated amount due
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Upgrade">
    Complete the payment to activate your new plan immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **EU Customers**: When upgrading to a paid plan, you'll be asked to acknowledge the withdrawal waiver for digital goods as required by EU consumer protection law.
</Note>

## Downgrading Your Plan

When you downgrade to a lower tier:

1. **End of Cycle**: Downgrade takes effect at your next billing date
2. **Feature Limits**: Features above your new limit become read-only
3. **Bot Limits**: Extra bots are automatically stopped (but not deleted)

<Warning>
  Make sure to adjust your configuration before downgrading. Bots beyond your new limit will be stopped automatically.
</Warning>

### What Happens to Your Data

| Item | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| Tickets | Remain accessible (read-only for closed tickets) |
| Transcripts | Always available regardless of plan |
| Custom Embeds | Excess embeds become read-only |
| Canned Responses | Excess responses become read-only |
| AI Tokens | Reset to new plan allocation |
| Knowledge Base | Sources beyond limit become inactive |
## Cancelling Your Subscription
You can cancel your subscription at any time:
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Dashboard → Profile → Billing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Select **Cancel Subscription** at the bottom of the page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Choose whether to cancel immediately or at end of billing period
  </Step>
</Steps>
### After Cancellation
- **Immediate Cancellation**: Account downgrades to Free immediately
- **End of Period**: You keep paid features until your billing period ends
- **Data Retention**: All your data (tickets, transcripts, settings) is preserved
- **Reactivation**: You can resubscribe at any time to regain access

## Updating Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Payment Settings">
    Go to Billing → **Payment Methods**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Card">
    Click **Add Payment Method** and enter your card details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as Default">
    Click the three dots next to the new card and select **Set as Default**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Old Card">
    Optionally remove your old payment method
  </Step>
</Steps>
<Tip>
  We recommend updating your payment method before your card expires to avoid service interruption.
</Tip>
## Applying Coupon Codes
Have a promotional code? Here's how to apply it:
<Steps>
  <Step title="During Checkout">
    When upgrading or starting a trial, look for **Have a coupon?** link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Code">
    Type your coupon code and click **Apply**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Discount">
    The discounted price will be shown before you complete payment
  </Step>
</Steps>
<Info>
  Coupon codes can only be applied once per account and may have expiration dates or usage limits.
</Info>
## Switching Billing Cycle
To switch between monthly and yearly billing:
1. Go to **Billing** → **Change Plan**
2. Select the same plan but different billing cycle
3. For monthly → yearly: Pay the yearly amount (prorated credit applied)
4. For yearly → monthly: Change takes effect at renewal

<Note>
  Switching from yearly to monthly does not provide a partial refund. The change applies when your yearly subscription renews.
</Note>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will I lose my data if I cancel?">
    No. Your tickets, transcripts, and settings are preserved even after cancellation. You can resubscribe anytime to regain access to paid features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    We don't currently offer subscription pausing. You can cancel and resubscribe later when needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my payment fails?">
    We'll retry the payment a few times and send you email notifications. If payment continues to fail, your account will be downgraded to Free after a grace period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    EU customers have a 14-day withdrawal right for unused services. For other refund requests, contact us at hi@ticketcord.net.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
