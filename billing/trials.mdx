## Starting a Trial

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Go to [ticketcord.net/dashboard](https://ticketcord.net/dashboard) and sign in with Discord
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Plan">
    Navigate to Billing and select **Basic**, **Pro**, or **Enterprise**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Trial">
    Click **Start Free Trial** - verify your card with €1 (refunded instantly)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Included

During your trial, you have **full access** to all features of your chosen plan:

### Basic Trial
- 3 custom bots
- Remove TicketCord branding
- Analytics dashboard
- 5 custom embeds
- 25 canned responses

### Pro Trial
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- AI-powered features with token allocation
- Atlas AI assistant
- Knowledge base (2 sources)
- Auto-routing, priority detection, and more

### Enterprise Trial
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- 6 custom bots
- 15 knowledge base sources
- Webhooks (20 per guild)
- SLA management
- Business hours configuration

## During Your Trial

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans during the trial?">
    Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade to a different trial plan at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I be charged automatically?">
    Only if you add a payment method. Without payment info, your account automatically downgrades to Free when the trial ends.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use all the AI tokens?">
    Yes, you receive the full monthly token allocation during your trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data after the trial?">
    All your tickets, transcripts, and settings are preserved. Only access to paid features is restricted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Converting to Paid

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Before your trial ends, go to Billing → **Add Payment Method**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Billing Cycle">
    Select monthly or yearly billing (save 17% with yearly)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Subscription">
    Complete the checkout to start your paid subscription
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When Your Trial Ends

### If You've Added Payment
- Your subscription automatically starts
- You're charged for your first billing period
- All features continue uninterrupted

### If You Haven't Added Payment
- Account automatically downgrades to Free plan
- Paid features become inaccessible
- Your data is preserved
- You can subscribe later to regain access
