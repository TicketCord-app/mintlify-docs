## What Happens When You Upgrade?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Instant Access">
    All new features are available immediately after payment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prorated Billing">
    If upgrading mid-cycle, you only pay the difference for remaining days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep Your Data">
    All your existing tickets, embeds, and settings are preserved
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When You Downgrade?

- Features above your new plan limit become read-only
- Extra bots beyond your limit are automatically stopped
- Existing tickets and transcripts remain accessible
- AI tokens reset to new plan allocation at next billing cycle

  <Accordion title="What counts as a 'bot'?">
    Each custom Discord bot you create counts as one bot. A bot can be connected to multiple Discord servers (guilds).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused AI tokens roll over?">
    No, AI tokens reset at the beginning of each billing cycle and do not roll over to the next month.
  </Accordion>
